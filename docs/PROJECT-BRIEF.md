# Project Brief

## Goal

Build a personal website that works as:

- a public writing archive
- a professional identity page
- an interview-friendly resume site
- a home for future AI application / indie project work

## Audience

Primary audiences:

- hiring managers
- interviewers
- technical peers
- potential collaborators
- readers coming from the WeChat account

## What The Site Must Communicate Fast

Within the first screen, a visitor should understand:

- who Alex is
- what kind of engineer he is
- what transition he is making
- what he writes about
- why this site is worth exploring

## Core Narrative

The site should reflect this path:

`10 年 Java 后端 -> 大厂裁员 -> 国企外包 -> 转 AI 应用和独立开发`

## Content Strategy

The site is not a dump of all internal writing assets.

Public content should be selected and polished.

The first public content pillars are:

- long-form essays about survival and transition in the AI era
- selected AI/tooling observations that connect back to programmer identity
- project pages that show actual engineering work
- structured resume / experience pages
